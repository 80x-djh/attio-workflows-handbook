This is the **learning path** — an ordered route from "I've never opened the workflow canvas" to "I run an AI agent that owns a stage of my funnel." It sits on top of the [reference](/reference/): the reference is the lookup layer; this is the curriculum that teaches you what to look up and why.

Every lesson follows the same rhythm: what you'll be able to do by the end, the job it solves, the mental model, the build with real blocks, and what it costs before you publish. Each applied track ends in a **Challenge** — a complete, follow-along build you ship into your own workspace.

## Start with the foundations

Before the funnel-stage tracks, learn the engine itself.

- [Build your first workflow](/learn/build-your-first-workflow/) — a free, logic-only Slack alert that touches every fundamental: trigger, filter, variables, an action, and publishing. About ten minutes, zero credits.

New to the concepts? The [Explanation](/explanation/) section is the "why" behind every block — [triggers](/explanation/triggers/), [variables](/explanation/variables/), [conditions](/explanation/conditions-and-filters/), [records vs list entries](/explanation/records-vs-list-entries/), and [how runs work](/explanation/runs-and-debugging/).

## The applied tracks

Four tracks, each mapped to a stage of the go-to-market motion. Work them in order or jump to the one that hurts most — each lists its own prerequisites and ends in a Challenge you ship.

1. [Capture and qualify](/learn/capture-and-qualify/) — make inbound arrive enriched, scored, and routable, paying for AI only where it changes a decision.
2. [Route and sequence](/learn/route-and-sequence/) — get the right rep on the right account at the right moment, with clean enroll/exit hygiene.
3. [Keep the pipeline honest](/learn/keep-the-pipeline-honest/) — make the pipeline self-clean, self-summarize, and hand off, on a schedule, with no false positives.
4. [Let AI own a stage](/learn/let-ai-own-a-stage/) — stand up a read-only research agent that runs a funnel segment safely and predictably.

Each track's Challenge is a complete build in [Guides](/guides/) — the curriculum graduates you straight into the how-to layer.
