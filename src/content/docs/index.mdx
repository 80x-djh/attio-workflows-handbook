## Why this handbook exists

Attio shipped a new Workflows engine in June 2026: AI agent blocks, custom JavaScript, MCP tool access, multi-trigger support, and a dramatically cheaper credit model. The official docs tell you what each block does. This handbook tells you **how to actually build with them** — and what breaks when you don't.

It's written by a consultant who builds Attio workspaces for VC funds and startups full-time, and it's open source: spot a gap, [open a PR](https://github.com/80x-djh/attio-workflows-handbook).
