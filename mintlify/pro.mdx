## Why this work is hard

Attio Workflows sit at the intersection of CRM design, go-to-market (GTM) operations, APIs, AI agents, and data hygiene. Clicking the blocks is rarely the hard part. The hard part is knowing which [trigger](/explanation/triggers) should exist, where the source of truth lives, which [path should stay free](/reference/credits-and-pricing), which write can cause an [infinite loop](/explanation/infinite-loops-and-safety), and which records should never reach an AI step. These offers cover that work.
