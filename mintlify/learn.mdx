New to Attio workflows? Learn starts you at your first build, then walks you through four guided paths in order, from logic-only alerts to AI agents. A workflow is an automation that reacts to something in your CRM and takes action.

Work this section top to bottom. First build one workflow end to end. Then follow the guided paths in order, or jump to the one that solves your biggest problem today.

This is the only section meant to be read in sequence. The other three answer one question at a time. Grab a finished recipe in [Guides](/guides). Look up a single block in [Reference](/reference). Understand why the engine behaves a certain way in [Explanation](/explanation).

## Guided paths

A guided path takes one job in your go-to-market motion and teaches it as a short course. It gives you the mental model first, then walks you through the real builds for that job, in order. The builds themselves live in [Guides](/guides), where each one stands alone. A path is the guided route through them, with the why and the sequence added.

Follow the four paths in order to go from your first alert to an AI agent that owns a stage of your funnel. Each path ends in a challenge you ship into your own workspace.
