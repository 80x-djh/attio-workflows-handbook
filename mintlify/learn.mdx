The Learn track teaches Attio workflows in order, from your first logic-only build to an AI agent that owns a stage of your funnel. A workflow is an automation that reacts to something in your CRM and takes action. The [reference](/reference) is the place to look up each piece. This track is the course that teaches you what to look up and why.

Start with the foundations. Then work the four applied tracks in order, or jump to the one that hurts most. Each applied track ends in a Challenge. A Challenge is a complete follow-along build that you ship into your own workspace from [Guides](/guides).
