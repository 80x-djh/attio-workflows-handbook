The Learn track teaches Attio workflows in order, from your first logic-only build to an AI agent that owns a stage of your funnel. It sits on top of the [reference](/reference): the reference is the lookup layer, and this is the curriculum that teaches you what to look up and why. Start with the foundations, then work the four applied tracks in order or jump to the one that hurts most. Each applied track ends in a Challenge — a complete, follow-along build you ship into your own workspace from [Guides](/guides).
