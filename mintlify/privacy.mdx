This site collects analytics, server logs, and an optional mailing list address. You can exercise your data-protection rights by emailing [dan@80x.ai](mailto:dan@80x.ai). It is run by Dan Hull (80x), an independent consultant, as a free documentation resource. It is not a data business.

**Last updated: June 11, 2026**

## What is collected

The site gathers two kinds of data. Both are used only to operate and improve the resource.

- **Analytics.** The site may use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymization. This shows aggregate usage. For example, it shows which pages are read, roughly where visitors come from, and what devices they use. Analytics may not be active yet. This policy covers it from the moment it is enabled. No analytics data is used to identify individual visitors.
- **Server and content-delivery logs.** The site is hosted on Vercel, which keeps standard server logs. These logs include your IP address, user agent, requested URL, and a timestamp. They are kept to operate and secure the service. For how long they are kept, see [Vercel's privacy policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## Cookies

When Google Analytics 4 is active, it sets cookies or similar identifiers. These tell one visitor apart from another. No other tracking runs on the site. There are no ad pixels, no fingerprinting, and no third-party advertising scripts.

## Mailing list

Joining the mailing list means you give your email address voluntarily. The address is used only to send handbook updates. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing takes effect immediately. Your address is never sold or shared for marketing.

## Third parties

Three processors handle data on the site's behalf.

- **Google** processes analytics when Google Analytics 4 is enabled.
- **Vercel** provides hosting and content delivery, including server logs.
- **GitHub** governs any activity on the [handbook repository](https://github.com/80x-djh/attio-workflows-handbook). Opening issues or pull requests there is subject to GitHub's own terms and privacy policy.

## Your rights

These rights apply if you are in the European Union, the European Economic Area, or the United Kingdom. You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. You can also object to or restrict its processing. To make any request, email [dan@80x.ai](mailto:dan@80x.ai), and it is handled directly. You can also complain to your local data protection authority.

## What is not done

No personal data is sold. The site shows no ads. Nothing is collected beyond what this policy describes.

## Changes

Any change to this policy is noted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
