Guides are complete, follow-along Attio workflow builds, each with the exact trigger, blocks, credit math, and a test plan you can run. Every build names its trigger, configures every block with real field values, shows the per-run credit cost, and ends with a test you fire yourself. Ship it into your own workspace and watch it run. They assume you have finished [Build your first workflow](/learn/build-your-first-workflow); when a concept is unclear, [How the engine works](/explanation) covers the why.

## What makes a good build

The best Attio workflow is boring. It pairs a precise trigger with an early, free filter, then a minimal data lookup, one clear write or external action, run-viewer verification, and a documented owner and rollback. Reach for agents, the [Send HTTP request](/reference/utilities/send-http-request) block, and the [Execute code](/reference/calculations/execute-code) block when they remove real complexity, not because they are available.
